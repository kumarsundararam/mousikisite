## ?? **Design Elements Applied to Completed Pages**

### Visual Components:
- ? Animated gradient heroes (hero-animated class)
- ? Wave SVG backgrounds
- ? Floating musical note animations
- ? Gradient text headers (WebkitBackgroundClip)
- ? Colorful hover effects
- ? Card lift animations (translateY)
- ? Star ratings in testimonials
- ? Music-themed emojis throughout

### Color Schemes:
- **Purple Gradient**: #667eea ? #764ba2 ? #f093fb (Teachers, Home)
- **Orange Gradient**: #f59e0b ? #ea580c ? #fb923c (Schools)
- **Green Gradient**: #22c55e ? #16a34a ? #10b981 (Students)
- **Pink Gradient**: #ec4899 ? #db2777 ? #f472b6 (Parents)

### Typography:
- Headers: font-weight 800, 2.5-3.5rem
- Subheaders: font-weight 700, 1.3-1.8rem
- Body: line-height 1.7-2.2
- Text shadows for depth

### Interactive:
- btn-creative class with hover effects
- btn-creative-outline for secondary actions
- Card hover: scale, shadow, translateY
- Icon hover: scale, rotate
- Smooth transitions: 0.3-0.4s ease
